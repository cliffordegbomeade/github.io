About Me

A Career Built on Purposeful Communication

I am a marketing and brand communications professional with extensive experience executing integrated communications strategies for leading organisations across Nigeria. My work spans public relations, digital marketing, corporate communications, and executive consulting — always grounded in strategy and driven by results.

Currently serving as MD/CEO of PSHAN, a health-focused NGO, I lead communications and stakeholder engagement at the intersection of public health, policy, and impact. I have advised brands in financial services, FMCG, ICT, entertainment, and the development sector.

Whether coordinating high-profile galas, launching multi-channel campaigns, or managing executive communications, I bring structure, creativity, and measurable outcomes to every engagement.
